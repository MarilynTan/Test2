Crunches

Step 1

Lie face up on the ground. Bend your knees and your feet are flat on the floor.

Step 2

Place your hands behind your head and lift your head and shoulders slightly off the floor.

Step 3

Go back to the lying position and start from step 1 again.

Instructions

00:



Plank is held in a push-up position with the body's weight borne on forearms, elbows, and toes.

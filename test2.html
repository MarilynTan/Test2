Plank

Step 1

Line up your shoulders directly over your hands and your hips directly over your knees.

Step 2

Move your body into the plank pose. Slowly raise yourself into a basic push-up position.

Step 3

Keep your thighs lifted. Your body should not curve, but rather should form a straight and balanced line. Align your body so that your shoulders are directly above your wrists, and hold it there.

Step 4

Hold it for five smooth breaths and then transition into the next position. If you are using planks to build strength and stamina, hold the pose for as long as you can. Start with 30-60 seconds.

Step 5

End the pose when you are done. Transition to another yoga position, return to your hands and knees, or lay down again to rest, prone.
